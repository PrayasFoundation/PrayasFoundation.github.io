Who We Are?

Prayas Foundation is for public donation center with blood donation members in the changing health care system.

This is a little effort as a platform to do such humanitarian work to save lives. This campaign, which has been going on continuously for more than 5 years, is helping not only in Uttar Pradesh but also in the other states of India. Blood donation is a divine experience. One unit of blood saves three lives. Donating blood may reduce the risk of heart disease and stimulate the generation of red blood cells. There is no substitute for human blood. It can be acquired only from a generous blood donor.

Our Purpose

Our main motiveis not only to donate and collect blood of different types, but also to increase awareness among people and to encourage them to realise how beautiful it is to save a a life. Prayas Foundation is an organization, is a group that is doing this humanitarian work through various social media platforms WhatsApp, Facebook, Twitter. Our purpose of blood donation camp to select a suitable donor whose blood will be safe to the recipient and who himself shall not in any way be harmed by blood donation.

How we help

The following are six steps of camp phase arrangements:

  • 1. Registration: Blood donor's personal detail such as name, father's name, age, address, telephone no., etc. is recorded and the questionnaire is given to donors to access their present and past health status. The purpose is to select a suitable donor whose blood will be safe for a recipient.
  • 2. Hemoglobin test: This has to be done in the blood donation camp itself and by the technician on duty before the donor is declared fit. Blood donor's hemoglobin level will be checked to ensure that he/she is not suffering from anemia and can safely donate a unit of blood.
  • 3. Medical examination: A medical officer asks the blood donor certain questions about his medical history to ascertain that blood donor is fit to donate blood and he will examine donors. Donor's weight, blood pressure, pulse and temperature are recorded. Only good health, mentally alert and physically fit individuals are accepted as blood donors.
  • 4. Donation of blood: On receiving the donor from the medical officer, check the identity of the donor with the donor form

  • 5. Refreshment: After donation, allow donor to sit in the refreshment area under observation and served with some light refreshments. The donors should be advised to remain in refreshment room for at least 15 minutes and should be advised to increase their water consumption
  • 6. Storage of blood: The blood bag is stored as per instructions, at correct temperatures.

How it all started

We started with a very small number of people donating blood in little camps and saw how beautiful itis to save a life. Then we realised to help out needy people and make a change in the world by giving it a name so that people are atleast aware of their resources. Hence here we are, established an organisation nation-wide and encouraging people to donate blood.
